My background

In 1994, a month before Kurt Cobain died, I was born. I've lived my life all over Finland, experiencing different environments and meeting different people from the start. In my life there have been to constant elements: 'change' and 'creation'.

I was four years old when I sat in front of the family computer.  I started to teach myself digital drawing, animation (RIP Flash), how to create a website, how to use Adobe's tools for my projects, I even created, hosted and admined a fansite with a phpBB-forum for the Finnish Dragonball- fans when I was in the 6th grade.

I've also worked blue collar and manual labour jobs from a young age, whether it'd be in the family farm or a summer job as a removalist. Applying the work ethic I learned doing bodily work to the stuff I was passionate about really clarified the path I wanted to take in life. For me, creating things with a computer as the weapon of my choice was so natural that when it was time to enroll for University I already knew where to apply. In 2013, I started studying Information Processing Sciences in University of Oulu (home of IRC!). Alongside my studies I did freelancing as a graphic designer to make some extra coin.

In 2016 I started working in OP Lab - R&D-unit of the biggest financial group in Finland.
